Video Production

Texas Tech Hype Video

Project Overview

This project involved concepting, scripting, and editing a high-energy promotional hype video for Texas Tech Athletics to celebrate the Red Raiders football program's historic 2025 season, which included the program's first Big 12 Championship and first College Football Playoff appearance.

The Challenge

The goal was to capture the excitement and emotion of a season that meant everything to Raider Nation, while keeping the video tight, punchy, and shareable on social media. The video needed to work as both a standalone season recap and a hype piece heading into the offseason.

My Role

I served as the primary video editor and script consultant. I was responsible for selecting key footage moments, writing the narrative voiceover structure, choosing the music, and assembling the final cut in Adobe Premiere Pro. I collaborated closely with the Athletics communications team on the final messaging.

Process

I started by reviewing over 40 hours of game footage, press conferences, and behind-the-scenes material from the 2025 season. From there I built a narrative arc that followed the team from early season expectations through the Big 12 title win and into the CFP. The final video runs approximately 3 minutes and 20 seconds and was designed to be emotionally resonant while keeping the pace fast.

Outcome

The video was shared across Texas Tech Athletics' social channels and received strong engagement from the Raider Nation fanbase. It served as the official season recap video for the program and was played at the Big 12 Championship ring ceremony in April 2026.

What I Learned

This project taught me how to manage large amounts of raw footage and find the emotional story within it. I also learned how pacing, music selection, and narrative structure all work together to create something that connects with an audience on a personal level.
