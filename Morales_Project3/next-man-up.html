Next Man Up

(Ted Lasso) — Spec Script
COLD OPEN INT. COACHES’ OFFICE – AFC RICHMOND – MORNING TED LASSO, COACH BEARD, and NATE are crowded around a laptop. Beard reads from the screen. COACH BEARD And that makes three. Jamie, Isaac, and Danny — all down with whatever plague this is. TED Dang. You know it’s bad when Danny Rojas isn’t bouncing off the walls yelling about life and football. NATE And Isaac said he has chills and hallucinated his own haircut fighting him. Ted winces. TED Alright, so, we’re playin’ Man City without our star striker, our captain, and our ray of sunshine. COACH BEARD (Deadpan) Perfect. Ted claps his hands, forcing a smile. TED Hey, fellas — challenge accepted, right? SMASH TO: SCENE 1 INT. LOCKER ROOM – AFC RICHMOND – LATER TED stands before the team. Most of the players look confused or slightly panicked. TED Alright, team. Got some tough news. Jamie, Isaac, and Danny are out for the count. Flu’s got ’em tighter than my Aunt Janice’s hugs at a family reunion. A beat. Silence. Confusion. TED (CONT’D) That means y’all are up. And I’m not worried one bit. SAM (softly) We’re playing Man City... TED Yup. Best team in the league. But guess what? Pressure makes diamonds. The team is quiet. TED (CONT’D) Or… pressure bursts pipes. Either way, you’re gonna make something explode out there. Some nervous chuckles. TED (CONT’D) Now let’s go make some diamond dust. SCENE 2 INT. SICK ROOM – AFC RICHMOND – SAME TIME DANNY ROJAS, pale but grinning, tiptoes past the door, wrapped in a blanket like a sickly superhero. DANNY (whispering) Football is life. Football is healing. He slowly peels the blanket off and sneaks down the hallway in full kit. SCENE 3 EXT. TRAINING PITCH – MOMENTS LATER DANNY jogs unsteadily onto the pitch. COACH BEARD spots him from across the field. COACH BEARD Oh come on — Beard storms over. COACH BEARD (CONT’D) Danny! You’re supposed to be in bed! DANNY I feel... 62 percent alive. That is enough! Danny kicks a ball. It goes 10 feet, then he wobbles. COACH BEARD Enough of this. Beard lifts Danny off the ground like a sack of flour and marches him back inside. DANNY Football is liiiiiiife — Reach Out if you are interested in more.
